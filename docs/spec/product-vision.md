# Product Vision & Scope

## Vision
To create an intelligent, integrated, and future-ready talent development ecosystem that empowers every associate to continuously learn, grow, demonstrate competency, achieve career aspirations, and contribute meaningfully to organizational success.

## Mission
To provide a unified platform that enables organizations to assess, develop, validate, and optimize workforce capabilities through personalized learning, competency-driven development, AI-powered recommendations, continuous assessments, career pathing, and workforce intelligence.

## Business Problems Being Solved

- No visibility into workforce skills and competencies across individual, team, project, practice, and org levels
- Fragmented systems for learning, assessments, certifications, career development, skill validation, and workforce planning
- No personalized learning journeys tied to career aspirations, competency gaps, and business priorities
- No way to measure learning effectiveness, applied learning, skill growth, competency maturity, or business impact
- Manual effort in training administration, competency management, approvals, feedback, validations, and reporting
- No integration between HRMS, learning, collaboration, certification, assessment, payroll, identity, project, and knowledge systems
- No support for career aspirations, internal mobility, succession readiness, knowledge reuse, or capability forecasting

## Product Purpose

- Establish a centralized competency and skill intelligence ecosystem
- Enable comprehensive workforce capability visibility and readiness planning
- Deliver personalized learning, career, and development experiences
- Validate learning through assessments, implementation evidence, capstones, POCs, innovation initiatives, and project work
- Preserve, organize, validate, and democratize organizational knowledge
- Enable career growth, internal mobility, certification attainment, recognition, incentives, and workforce intelligence
- Support configurable workflows, role-based access, shared governance, auditability, and future SaaS evolution

## Design Principles

| Principle | What it means in code |
|---|---|
| AI-First | AI supports recommendations, tutoring, content generation, assessment assistance, analytics. Human review required where outcomes affect people. |
| Competency-Driven | Learning, assessments, certifications, career paths, dev plans, and workforce planning are all mapped to competencies and proficiency levels. |
| Personalized Experience | Platform adapts to role, aspirations, learning history, skill gaps, project needs, preferences, and org priorities. |
| Applied Learning First | Completion alone does NOT imply competency. Practical evidence and validation may be required. |
| Configurable, Not Hardcoded | Workflows, business rules, roles, scoring, statuses, paths, visibility, recommendations, and incentives are ALL configurable. |
| SaaS-Ready Architecture | Begins internally but must evolve into multi-tenant. Build tenant-aware from day one. |
| Role-Based Experience | Dashboards, reports, workflows, access, and navigation are personalized by role and visibility scope. |
| Workflow Consistency | All approvals use: approve, reject, send back, request info, comments, attachments, audit trail, escalation, delegation. |
| Audit by Design | Learning, assessments, certifications, approvals, config, access, AI interactions, and governance actions are ALL auditable. |
| Integration First | HRMS, Outlook, Teams, identity providers, payroll, cert providers, assessment engines, Git, labs, and knowledge stores are contract-driven integrations. |
| Mobile First | Responsive and mobile-friendly for learners, approvers, mentors, trainers, and managers. |

## Success Metrics

- Learning completion rate and overdue learning rate
- Competency growth rate and validated skill growth rate
- Certification attainment rate and certification expiry risk
- Skill gap reduction at individual, team, project, practice, and org levels
- Strategic Skill Readiness Index
- Learning-to-Implementation Conversion Rate
- Knowledge Contribution Index and Knowledge Reuse Rate
- User adoption, active usage, search success, recommendation click-through, learner satisfaction
- Internal mobility growth and career readiness improvement
